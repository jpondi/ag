 Jesus is Alive today (2)
 Jesus is Alive, very much Alive,
 Jesus is Alive Today.

[V1]
 He's living in the hearts of
 ordinary people,
 Helping them to love both
 God and man.
 Giving them the faith to
 look for miracles,
 Helping them to know
 they count in His plan.

[V2]
 He's always doing
 things He used to do;
 Things like making people
 clean and new.
 Even though He's got a
 world to look after,
 Still He has the time to
 answer prayer too.
